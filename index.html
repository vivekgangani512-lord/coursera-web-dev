Our Menu

Dosa

A thin, crispy South Indian crepe made from fermented rice and lentil batter, cooked golden and served with chutney and sambar.

Idly

Soft, steamed rice cakes made from a fermented batter of rice and lentils, light on the stomach and traditionally served with coconut chutney and sambar.

Menduvada

Crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, these savory lentil fritters are deep-fried and served hot with chutney and sambar.
